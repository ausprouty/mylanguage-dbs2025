How to target logs

One file (and its sub-namespaces):

URL: ?debug=InterfaceService*

Or console (persists):
localStorage.DEBUG = 'InterfaceService*'; location.reload();

A single sub-namespace only:

?debug=InterfaceService:net

Multiple files/namespaces:

?debug=InterfaceService*,MenuService,ShareLink

Everything (dev firehose):

?debug=* or localStorage.DEBUG='*'

Disable:

localStorage.removeItem('DEBUG'); location.reload();

Patterns use * as a wildcard and are comma-separated, case-insensitive.
